Model Architecture & Pipeline

Discover the data workflow, feature configuration, and mathematical regressor behind StockPredict AI's forecasting system.

Data Engineering Workflow

StockPredict AI runs a structured ML pipeline every time a query is executed. The engine pulls raw arrays, runs calculations on rolling windows, and fits a high-dimensional random forest to project upcoming price coordinates.

01

Historical Ingestion

The backend connects to Yahoo Finance to retrieve 2 full years of daily raw equity data, capturing Open, High, Low, Close, and Volume vectors.

02

Technical Oscillators

Calculates daily percentage returns alongside 5-day (short-term) and 20-day (trend) moving averages and annualized historical volatility.

03

Feature Engineering

Features are aligned with targets using standard shifting. Data vectors are scaled through MinMaxScaler to optimize random forest splits.

04

Forest Regression

Trains a Random Forest Regressor of 100 decision trees. Deep-learning depth bounds are set at 10 to prevent overfitting.

Model Safety & Limitations

Educational Disclaimer: Equity and derivative trading involves substantial capital risk. The Random Forest model executes historical curve fitting on technical indicators and does not compute real-world parameters such as macroeconomic conditions, microeconomic changes, unexpected earnings reports, news events, or black swan occurrences. This tool is designed purely for analytics demonstrations and should not be used as professional financial advice.
